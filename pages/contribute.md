There are several ways you can contribute to our website, including reporting issues, making donations, and writing articles. Below are the details for each method of contribution.


## Donations

If you would like to support our work, we welcome donations. Your contributions help us maintain and improve our site.

You can donate through the following methods:
- **PayPal**: [Donate via PayPal](https://paypal.me/bandithijo)
- **Saweria**: [Support us on Saweria](https://saweria.co/bandithijo)


## Writing Articles

If you are interested in writing articles for our site, please follow these guidelines:

1. **Choose a Topic**: Select a topic that you are passionate about and that aligns with our site's focus.
2. **Research**: Ensure your information is accurate and up-to-date.
3. **Draft Your Article**: Write a clear and concise article, keeping in mind our audience.
4. **Fork our Repo**: Fork our repository on GitHub, add your article in the appropriate directory.
5. **Submit for Review**: Once your article is ready, submit it for review by creating a pull request.
6. **Revisions**: Make any necessary revisions based on feedback received.
7. **Publication**: Once approved, your article will be published on our site.

This is the guidance page for contributing to our website, "[Writing Rules]({{ '/writing-rules/' | absolute_url }})". If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us via our contact page.

We appreciate your contributions and look forward to collaborating with you!
